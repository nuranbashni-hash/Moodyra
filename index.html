                      💡 Exam Stress Relief Tips

                      📖

                      Review, don't re-read

                      Close your book and try to recall key points first. Only look when you're stuck.

                      🧩

                      Practice past papers

                      Past exam questions are the best predictor of what's coming. Time yourself.

                      🗣️

                      Teach it out loud

                      Explain the topic as if teaching a friend. If you can teach it, you know it.

                      💤

                      Sleep before the exam

                      Memory consolidates during sleep. A rested brain outperforms an all-nighter.

                      🥤

                      Stay hydrated

                      Dehydration reduces focus by up to 20%. Keep a water bottle at your desk.

                      ✅

                      Done is better than perfect

                      Attempt every question. A partial answer is always better than a blank.

                      About Moodyra 🌈

                      A mood-based wellness website for students

                      🎯

                      Our Goal

                      Help students recognize and manage their emotions through personalized content, music, and study tools.

                      🌈

                      5 Moods

                      Each mood — Happy, Sad, Calm, Angry, Tired — has its own page with matching colors, quotes, and music.

                      📚

                      Student Tools

                      Focus timer, breathing exercises, study tips by mood, and quick access to ChatGPT for study help.

                      🔒

                      Privacy First
